About Total HVAC of Michigan

Your trusted partner for all HVAC needs since serving Michigan

Who We Are

We are a Michigan HVAC company delivering dependable repairs, maintenance, and our new HVAC Business‑as‑a‑Service program for homeowners and businesses. With years of expertise and a commitment to excellence, we've become the trusted choice for heating and cooling solutions across Michigan.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, professional HVAC services that keep our customers comfortable year-round while offering innovative solutions like our Business-as-a-Service model that prioritizes customer satisfaction and value.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We show up on time and deliver quality work every time
  • Expertise: Our technicians are highly trained and certified professionals
  • Customer Focus: Your comfort and satisfaction is our top priority
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies like our BaaS program
  • Integrity: Transparent pricing and honest recommendations

What We Offer

  • Emergency HVAC Repairs
  • Regular Maintenance Plans
  • System Installation & Replacement
  • HVAC Business-as-a-Service (BaaS)
